# Wheat Pest Management (India)

## Aphids (Rhopalosiphum padi)
- Typical signs: curling leaves, sticky honeydew, black sooty mold.
- Risk window: late vegetative to grain filling when weather is mild.
- Threshold idea: check 20 tillers at 5 spots before spray decisions.

## Integrated Management
1. Field scouting twice per week in early morning.
2. Avoid excess nitrogen in single heavy dose.
3. Keep border weeds under control to reduce alternate hosts.
4. Use yellow sticky traps for monitoring.

## Chemical Use Safety
- Rotate mode of action to delay resistance.
- Spray in low wind periods.
- Respect pre-harvest interval on product label.
- Follow local agricultural extension guidance.
