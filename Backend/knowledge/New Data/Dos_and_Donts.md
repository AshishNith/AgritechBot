# Dos and Donts

| ✅ ❌  Farmer's Dos & Don'ts Guide |  |  |  |
| # | Product | ✅ Dos | ❌ Don'ts |
| 1.0 | Pretilachlor 50 EC | • Apply within 3–5 days of transplanting • Maintain 2–3 cm standing water • Use correct dose per acre | • Do not apply in dry soil • Do not spray after weed emergence • Avoid contact with skin/eyes |

| 10.0 | Dhaman | • Spray at 2–4 leaf weed stage • Use flat fan nozzle for uniform coverage • Apply in calm weather | • Do not spray on target crop leaves • Do not apply near sensitive crops • Do not apply in windy conditions |
