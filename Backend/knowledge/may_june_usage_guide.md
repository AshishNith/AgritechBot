## Detailed Product Guidelines

### 1. Pretilachlor 50 EC
* **Category:** Herbicide (Pre-emergent)
* **Target Crop:** Paddy / Rice
* **Pest / Purpose:** Controls grassy weeds & broadleaf weeds in rice fields.
* **Dose per Acre:** 400–500 ml diluted in 200 Litres of water.
* **Application Method:** Foliar spray / soil drench in fields with 2–3 cm of standing water.
* **Time of Application:** Within 3–5 days after transplanting (DAT).
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 60 days.
* **Precautions:** Do not spray in dry soil. Always wear Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). Avoid drift to neighboring non-target crops.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Pretilachlor 50 EC Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.0)

### 2. Pendimethalin 30 EC
* **Category:** Herbicide (Pre-emergent)
* **Target Crops:** Paddy, Maize, Soybean, Cotton, Vegetables
* **Pest / Purpose:** Prevents and controls annual grasses & broadleaf weeds before they sprout.
* **Dose per Acre:** 1.0–1.3 Litres dissolved in 200 Litres of water.
* **Application Method:** Soil spray evenly before weed emergence.
* **Time of Application:** Within 2 days of sowing (pre-emergence).
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 90 days.
* **Precautions:** Wear gloves and face mask during mixing and spraying. Avoid contact with eyes. Do not apply on waterlogged or flooded soil.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Pendimethalin 30 EC Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.ksymk014kjkq)

### 3. Cartap Hydrochloride 4% GR (4G)
* **Category:** Insecticide (Granule)
* **Target Crop:** Paddy / Rice
* **Pest / Purpose:** Controls Stem borer, Leaf folder, and Gall midge.
* **Dose per Acre:** 8–10 kg per acre.
* **Application Method:** Broadcast granules directly into standing water (2–3 cm).
* **Time of Application:** At the crop tillering stage (20–25 Days After Transplanting).
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 21 days.
* **Precautions:** Highly toxic to fish and aquatic life. Do not apply near active water bodies or fish ponds. Wear rubber gloves when handling.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Cartap Hydrochloride 4% GR Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.69sw9umh1rx7)

### 4. Fipronil 0.3 GR
* **Category:** Insecticide (Granule)
* **Target Crops:** Paddy, Sugarcane, Maize
* **Pest / Purpose:** Suppresses and controls White grub, Root aphid, Termite, and Stem borer.
* **Dose per Acre:** 8–10 kg per acre.
* **Application Method:** Broadcast or band application directly in the soil.
* **Time of Application:** At the time of sowing/transplanting, or at the first sign of pest infestation.
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 60 days.
* **Precautions:** Highly toxic to bees. Do not apply during active flowering stages to protect pollinators. Keep children and animals away from treated fields.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Fipronil 0.3 GR Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.f1xv78020xo)

### 5. Biozyme Power Plus GR
* **Category:** Bio-Stimulant / Growth Promoter
* **Target Crops:** All crops including Paddy, Cotton, Vegetables, and Pulses.
* **Pest / Purpose:** Enhances root system development, optimizes nutrient uptake, and boosts overall crop vigour.
* **Dose per Acre:** 4–5 kg per acre, mixed with sand or soil for uniform distribution.
* **Application Method:** Broadcast in the field or drip application.
* **Time of Application:** Apply as a basal dressing or during the transplanting stage.
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** No PHI (organic biological product).
* **Precautions:** Store in a cool, dry place. Use before the validity date. No mixing with strong chemical pesticides/fertilizers is required.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Biozyme Power Plus GR Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.rvpbdscv65dh)

### 6. Sulfino Gold
* **Category:** Fungicide + Sulfur-based Nutrition
* **Target Crops:** Paddy, Wheat, Vegetables, Fruits
* **Pest / Purpose:** Protects against Powdery mildew, Rust, Leaf spot, and Scab.
* **Dose per Acre:** 500 g to 1 kg per acre, diluted in 200 Litres of water.
* **Application Method:** Foliar spray.
* **Time of Application:** At the first visual sign of disease, or preventively every 15 days.
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 7 days.
* **Precautions:** Avoid spraying in extreme hot sun (temperatures above 35°C). Do not mix with alkaline chemicals.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Sulfino Gold Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.txaf9cymns4l)

### 7. Amaze XL GR
* **Category:** Insecticide (Granule)
* **Target Crops:** Paddy, Sugarcane, Maize, Vegetables
* **Pest / Purpose:** Suppresses soil pests like White grub, Root maggot, Cutworm, and Termite.
* **Dose per Acre:** 8–10 kg per acre.
* **Application Method:** Broadcast granules in moist soil before planting.
* **Time of Application:** At land preparation or right at transplanting.
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 30–45 days.
* **Precautions:** Keep away from natural water sources. Wear hand gloves. Do not inhale chemical dust.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Amaze XL GR Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.9hayr4z18cmi)

### 8. German King GR
* **Category:** Insecticide / Nematicide (Granule)
* **Target Crops:** Paddy, Vegetables, Pulses, Cotton
* **Pest / Purpose:** Targets soil-borne insects, nematodes, and white grub.
* **Dose per Acre:** 8–10 kg per acre.
* **Application Method:** Soil incorporation or broadcasting.
* **Time of Application:** Before sowing or at transplanting time.
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 30 days.
* **Precautions:** Wear a mask while broadcasting. Wash hands thoroughly with soap after use. Store away from food items.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [German King GR Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.3xndqjrkftj8)

### 9. Vasudha
* **Category:** Soil Conditioner / Bio-fertilizer
* **Target Crops:** All field crops, vegetables, and fruits.
* **Pest / Purpose:** Enhances soil structural health, boosts microbial activity, and improves soil nutrient availability.
* **Dose per Acre:** 4–5 kg per acre.
* **Application Method:** Mix with farm yard manure (FYM) or compost and broadcast.
* **Time of Application:** During field preparation or as a basal dressing.
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** No PHI (biological organic product).
* **Precautions:** Do not mix with chemical fertilizers. Use fresh stock.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Vasudha Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.evxrye5w6vyc)

### 10. Dhaman
* **Category:** Herbicide
* **Target Crops:** Paddy, Wheat, Sugarcane
* **Pest / Purpose:** Provides broad-spectrum weed control targeting grassy & broad-leaf weeds.
* **Dose per Acre:** 300–400 ml diluted in 150–200 Litres of water.
* **Application Method:** Foliar spray.
* **Time of Application:** Apply at the 2–4 leaf stage of weeds (early post-emergence).
* **Safety Interval (PHI):** 30 days.
* **Precautions:** Highly selective; avoid drift on non-target crops. Use a flat-fan nozzle for uniform soil coverage.
* **Available Months:** May–June
* **Source Document:** [Dhaman Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tuEwos_J5ddWACcec_lVijYlz1jXDkq1SaPZIkpyatQ/edit?tab=t.ec9dok1mlrzb)
