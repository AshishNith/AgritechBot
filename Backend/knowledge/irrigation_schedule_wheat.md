# Wheat Irrigation Scheduling Guide

## Critical Growth Stages
1. Crown root initiation (CRI): around 20-25 days after sowing.
2. Tillering.
3. Late jointing / booting.
4. Flowering.
5. Milking / dough stage.

## Practical Water Advice
- CRI irrigation is highest priority for yield.
- Use field moisture checks before each irrigation.
- Avoid waterlogging to prevent root stress.
- In sandy soils, prefer lighter and more frequent irrigation.

## Low-Cost Moisture Check
- Take soil from 10-15 cm depth.
- If soil does not form a weak ball in hand, irrigation is likely needed.
- Re-check in multiple field spots before decision.
